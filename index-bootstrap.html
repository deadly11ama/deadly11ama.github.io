Community Response

Community Response, a\ GovHack 2015 project. It helps communities help themselves in the aftermath of a natural disaster. We are the River Hacktivists.

Datasets Used: LINZ NZ Street Address (Electoral)

We use the LINZ New Zealand Electoral Street Address database. When users give us their street address, we find it in the LINZ database. Then we know their geographical location, and the official name for that location. This enables us to calculate distances and list nearby users in order of distance.
